How to use the compound interest calculator?


  • Fill in the initial value field;
  • Fill in the monthly value field (It will be added at the end of each cycle);
  • Fill in the annual or monthly interest rate;
  • Fill in the period field by months or years;
  • Click calculate and see the result.

**If all fields are not filled in, the result will not appear.**

How does the compound interest formula work?


Compound interest or simply interest on interest is calculated using the following formula:

To better understand the formula, follow the explanation of each variable:


A = P (1 + i) ^ t

  • A = final value, that is, the result you will have;
  • P = initial amount deposited;
  • i = interest rate;
  • t = investment time.

It is important to remember that the interest rate and time must be in the same unit.
